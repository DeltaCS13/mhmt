    Introduction to Jade Stones Massage for Advanced Stones Practitioners

    8 CEs

    In a market flooded with massage therapists, stand out from the crowd and offer something truly unique. Be one of the first therapists in the area to offer Jade Stones Massage! The smooth-as-glass jade stones have been carved into profoundly useful massage tools that offer tremendous therapeutic value to your clients while minimizing stress on therapist hands.

    The jade stones have a marvelous energy and feel to them, heat up faster than basalt, retain heat and cold equally well, and are fun to work with! Stone shapes include convex ovals, flat ovals, cylinders, spheres, crescent moons, and contoured toe stones. Each stone is uniquely beautiful, comfortable to use, and carries its own therapeutic benefit. Get excited about stones massage again!

    We will cover:

    • Set up, Heating, and Care of Stones
    • Contraindications and Safety
    • Physical and Subtle Properties of Jade
    • How to use the different stone shapes
    • Plenty of Hands-on time to practice what you learn
    • Pink Himalayan Sea Salt Massage Stones will also be introduced

    Stone sets are provided for the class and purchase of stones is NOT required to participate. However, stone sets will be available for purchase for when you fall in love with working with them.
