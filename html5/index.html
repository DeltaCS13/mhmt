  • Welcome to MHMT

    We provide bodywork to help your body work better.
    Whether you're recovering from an accident or injury, are looking to relax, or treat yourself to the pampering you so deserve, we're here for you. Every time you visit, your treatment is customized to meet your specific needs that day. Let us help you reduce your stress, untie your knots, relax your muscles, loosen what's tight, tighten what's loose, align what's crooked, balance what's off-kilter, and send you back to your life calm, relaxed, comfortable, at ease, clearer, more focused, more flexible, with less pain, able to rest - in a word, better. Imagine how amazing "better" will feel.

    About Our Therapist

    VIRGINIA LA FLAMME
    State Licensed (#5301) and Nationally Certified Massage Therapist.
    Virginia loves being a massage therapist and is eager to put her skills and experience to work for you. She is licensed in the state of North Carolina and holds national certification. She began her massage therapy training in 1995, graduating with academic honors, and has practiced in a variety of settings including salons, chiropractors offices, and was owner/operator of Spa Rubenesque - a small day spa focused on the unique needs of women of size. In 2005, she was recruited to join the team at the Spa at the Grove Park Inn, a local world renowned mega-spa, serving as a Massage Therapist and Aromatherapy Massage Trainer.
